How it works

Three ways to
make an impact

Whether you have an hour to give or an honest need to share, Karma meets you where you are.

1 🙏
Ask without shame

Need company, a listener, or a hand? Post an honest ask to your local community. Asking for help takes courage — and Karma rewards it.

2 🫶
Give what you can

Volunteer to help a neighbour. Log a kind act you did today — big or small. Every act earns karma points and inspires someone else to do the same.

3 🎁
Earn real rewards

Spend your karma at local businesses that believe in community — from yoga classes to cinema tickets to shared suppers. Kindness becomes currency.

What's inside

Built for real life,
not for likes.

No follower counts. No viral moments. Just a quiet, warm space to be human with the people around you.

Hyperlocal
Your street. Your people.

Karma shows you requests and acts from neighbours within walking distance. The closer the connection, the more real the help feels.

Community
Gather in real spaces

Host or join local events — coffee mornings, park walks, supper clubs, board game evenings. Earn karma just for showing up.

Safe connections
Chat, then meet

Every person has a verified profile. Message before you commit. Our community code of conduct means you're never meeting a stranger cold.

Private by design
Share only what you choose

Log acts just for yourself, or share them with your neighbourhood. No performance required. The karma you earn is real either way — and it's yours to keep.
